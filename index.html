A mobile game, fast paced, pick up and play

The objective is to tag every enemy on screen with a limited amount of time, with only one continuous line. As the game progresses, more enemies are likely to spawn, and the faster you'll have to be, while maintaining precision. I constructed the AI, and random generation to not continuously destroy enemy objects, as Unity has performance issues if you fill up it's "trash can", so the game uses page pooled enemy spawning.
